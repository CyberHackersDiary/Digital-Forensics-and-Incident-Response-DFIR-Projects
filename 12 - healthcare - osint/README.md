## Investigation Methodology

1. Define the healthcare or research question.
2. Collect relevant publicly available information.
3. Assess the credibility of sources.
4. Verify findings using scientific and authoritative references.
5. Correlate evidence across multiple sources.
6. Document conclusions with supporting evidence.
