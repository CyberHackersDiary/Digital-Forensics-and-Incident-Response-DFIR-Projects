Documenting investigative processes, findings, methodologies, and conclusions in a clear, structured, and evidence-based manner.

This repository documents my journey in professional investigation reporting through practical case studies, reporting frameworks, and real-world analysis.

## Investigation Methodology

1. Define the investigation scope and objectives.
2. Collect and organize evidence.
3. Analyze findings and supporting information.
4. Structure findings into a logical narrative.
5. Present evidence and conclusions clearly.
6. Produce a professional investigative report.
