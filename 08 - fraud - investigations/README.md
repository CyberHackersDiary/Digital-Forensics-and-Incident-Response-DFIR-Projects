## Investigation Methodology

1. Identify the suspected fraud activity.
2. Collect relevant publicly available information.
3. Analyze indicators of deception.
4. Verify claims and evidence.
5. Correlate findings across multiple sources.
6. Document conclusions with supporting evidence.
