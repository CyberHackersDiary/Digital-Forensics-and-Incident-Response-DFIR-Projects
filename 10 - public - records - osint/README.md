# 📋 Public Records OSINT

> Collecting, analysing, and verifying publicly available records to support digital investigations and intelligence gathering — from a single information requirement to a fully corroborated, evidence-backed intelligence finding.

Every investigation in this folder starts with an identified information need and ends with a documented, reproducible public records intelligence assessment. No assumptions. No shortcuts. Evidence in, intelligence out.

---

## 🎯 What This Covers

| Technique | What It Involves |
|---|---|
| **Public Records Research** | Systematically locating and extracting intelligence from publicly accessible record sources |
| **Government Databases** | Accessing and analysing official government datasets, registries, and open data portals |
| **Business Registrations** | Investigating company registration records, filing histories, and corporate status data |
| **Legal Records** | Researching publicly available legal filings, regulatory actions, and enforcement records |
| **Property Records** | Examining land registry, ownership history, and property transaction data |
| **Court Records** | Locating and analysing publicly accessible court filings, judgements, and case histories |
| **Academic & Research Records** | Investigating published research, institutional affiliations, and academic credentials |
| **Information Verification** | Cross-referencing public record findings against independent and authoritative sources |
| **Source Correlation** | Combining records from multiple public sources to build a comprehensive intelligence picture |
| **Evidence Documentation** | Recording all findings in structured, verifiable, and reproducible investigation reports |

## 🔬 Investigative Methodology

Each investigation follows a disciplined, repeatable eight-stage workflow:

```
╔══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║               INVESTIGATIVE METHODOLOGY                      ║
╠══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╣
║                                                              ║
║  01  RESEARCH QUESTION                                       ║
║      Define the information requirement and the records      ║
║      needed to answer it                                     ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  02  COLLECTION                                              ║
║      Locate and gather relevant public records from          ║
║      government portals, registries, and open databases      ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  03  VERIFICATION                                            ║
║      Validate source credibility and record authenticity     ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  04  CORRELATION                                             ║
║      Cross-reference findings across multiple public         ║
║      record sources and independent data sets                ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  05  ANALYSIS                                                ║
║      Apply public records research techniques to extract     ║
║      intelligence and establish contextual meaning           ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  06  FINDINGS                                                ║
║      Establish key intelligence conclusions supported        ║
║      by verified public record evidence                      ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  07  INTELLIGENCE ASSESSMENT                                 ║
║      Assess confidence level, source limitations, and        ║
║      alternative interpretations of the record evidence      ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  08  REPORT                                                  ║
║      Document conclusions in a structured, reproducible      ║
║      public records intelligence report                      ║
║                                                              ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

Every conclusion is evidence-backed. Every step is reproducible.

## ⚠️ Responsible Use

All investigations in this folder are conducted strictly using open-source intelligence and publicly available records:

- **Legal compliance** — All methods comply with applicable laws, access conditions, and platform terms of service
- **Public data only** — No unauthorised access to restricted, sealed, or confidential records
- **Privacy by design** — Records research is limited to what is necessary and proportionate to the investigation
- **Responsible handling** — Sensitive findings are handled with appropriate care and discretion
- **Educational purpose** — All case studies are conducted for learning and portfolio demonstration only
