# Public Records OSINT

Collecting, analyzing, and verifying publicly available records to support digital investigations and intelligence gathering through evidence-based research.

This repository documents my journey in Public Records OSINT through practical case studies, investigative methodologies, and real-world analysis.

## Areas of Focus

* Public Records Research
* Government Databases
* Business Registrations
* Legal Records
* Property Records
* Court Records
* Academic & Research Records
* Information Verification
* Source Correlation
* Evidence Documentation & Reporting

## Investigation Methodology

1. Identify the information requirement.
2. Locate relevant public records sources.
3. Collect publicly available records.
4. Verify information across multiple sources.
5. Correlate findings and establish context.
6. Document conclusions with supporting evidence.
