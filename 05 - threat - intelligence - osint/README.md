# Threat Intelligence

Investigating cyber threats, threat actors, malicious infrastructure, and emerging cyber risks using open-source intelligence and evidence-based analysis.

This repository documents my journey in threat intelligence through practical case studies, investigative methodologies, and real-world analysis.

## Areas of Focus

* Threat Actor Profiling
* Infrastructure Analysis
* Campaign Tracking
* Malware Intelligence
* Domain Intelligence
* Indicators of Compromise (IOCs)
* Attribution Analysis
* Threat Landscape Monitoring
* Intelligence Reporting
* Evidence Documentation & Reporting

## Investigation Methodology

1. Identify the threat or threat actor.
2. Collect publicly available intelligence.
3. Analyze indicators and infrastructure.
4. Correlate findings across multiple sources.
5. Assess risks and implications.
6. Document conclusions with supporting evidence.
