# 🕵️ Threat Intelligence

> Investigating cyber threats, threat actors, malicious infrastructure, and emerging cyber risks using open-source intelligence and evidence-based analysis — from a single indicator to a fully profiled threat campaign.

Every investigation in this folder starts with an identified threat or suspicious indicator and ends with a documented, reproducible intelligence assessment. No assumptions. No shortcuts. Evidence in, intelligence out.

---

## 🎯 What This Covers

| Technique | What It Involves |
|---|---|
| **Threat Actor Profiling** | Building intelligence profiles on adversaries using open-source tools and public data |
| **Infrastructure Analysis** | Mapping malicious domains, IPs, hosting providers, and network infrastructure |
| **Campaign Tracking** | Identifying and following coordinated threat campaigns across multiple indicators |
| **Malware Intelligence** | Analysing malware samples, behaviours, and associated indicators through open sources |
| **Domain Intelligence** | Investigating domain registration, history, DNS records, and associated infrastructure |
| **Indicators of Compromise (IOCs)** | Collecting, validating, and contextualising IOCs to support threat detection and response |
| **Attribution Analysis** | Assessing the origin, identity, and motivation of threat actors using available evidence |
| **Threat Landscape Monitoring** | Tracking emerging threats, vulnerabilities, and adversary activity across open sources |
| **Intelligence Reporting** | Producing structured threat intelligence reports for operational and strategic use |
| **Evidence Documentation** | Recording all findings in structured, verifiable, and reproducible investigation reports |

## 🔬 Investigative Methodology

Each investigation follows a disciplined, repeatable eight-stage workflow:

```
╔══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║               INVESTIGATIVE METHODOLOGY                      ║
╠══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╣
║                                                              ║
║  01  RESEARCH QUESTION                                       ║
║      Define the threat, target, or intelligence requirement  ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  02  COLLECTION                                              ║
║      Gather IOCs, infrastructure data, threat reports,       ║
║      and publicly available intelligence                     ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  03  VERIFICATION                                            ║
║      Validate source credibility and indicator integrity     ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  04  CORRELATION                                             ║
║      Cross-reference indicators across multiple platforms    ║
║      and independent threat intelligence sources             ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  05  ANALYSIS                                                ║
║      Apply threat intelligence techniques to profile         ║
║      actors, map infrastructure, and track campaigns         ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  06  FINDINGS                                                ║
║      Establish key threat intelligence conclusions with      ║
║      supporting evidence                                     ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  07  INTELLIGENCE ASSESSMENT                                 ║
║      Assess confidence level, attribution confidence,        ║
║      risk implications, and alternative hypotheses           ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  08  REPORT                                                  ║
║      Document conclusions in a structured, reproducible      ║
║      threat intelligence report                              ║
║                                                              ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

Every conclusion is evidence-backed. Every step is reproducible.

## ⚠️ Responsible Use

All investigations in this folder are conducted strictly using open-source intelligence and publicly available data:

- **Legal compliance** — All methods comply with applicable laws and platform terms of service
- **Public data only** — No unauthorised access, exploitation, or interaction with live threat infrastructure
- **Responsible handling** — Sensitive indicators and findings are handled with appropriate care and discretion
- **Educational purpose** — All case studies are conducted for learning and portfolio demonstration only
