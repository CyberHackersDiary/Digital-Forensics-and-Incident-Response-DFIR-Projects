# 🎬 Video Investigations

> Analysing and verifying digital videos through frame-by-frame examination, geolocation, timeline reconstruction, and systematic evidence-based investigation — from raw footage to a verified intelligence finding.

Every investigation in this folder starts with unverified video footage and ends with a documented, reproducible verification finding. No assumptions. No shortcuts. Evidence in, intelligence out.

---

## 🎯 What This Covers

| Technique | What It Involves |
|---|---|
| **Frame-by-Frame Analysis** | Extracting and examining individual frames to identify embedded visual indicators |
| **Video Geolocation** | Pinpointing filming locations using visual clues, satellite imagery, and mapping tools |
| **Timeline Verification** | Establishing and confirming when footage was recorded using contextual and technical evidence |
| **Event Reconstruction** | Piecing together the sequence of events depicted in footage from multiple sources |
| **Context Verification** | Validating whether footage accurately represents the event or claim it accompanies |
| **Metadata Analysis** | Examining embedded video metadata for device, location, and timestamp data |
| **Source Verification** | Tracing the original upload, publication history, and chain of custody of footage |
| **Audio & Visual Clue Analysis** | Extracting location, time, and contextual indicators from both audio and visual content |
| **Misinformation Detection** | Identifying footage used to spread false narratives or misrepresent events |
| **Evidence Documentation** | Recording all findings in structured, verifiable, and reproducible investigation reports |

## 🔬 Investigative Methodology

Each investigation follows a disciplined, repeatable eight-stage workflow:

```
╔══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║               INVESTIGATIVE METHODOLOGY                      ║
╠══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╣
║                                                              ║
║  01  RESEARCH QUESTION                                       ║
║      Define what needs to be verified and why                ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  02  COLLECTION                                              ║
║      Obtain the highest-quality version of the footage       ║
║      and all available surrounding context                   ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  03  VERIFICATION                                            ║
║      Validate source credibility and footage integrity       ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  04  CORRELATION                                             ║
║      Cross-reference findings across multiple independent    ║
║      sources and platforms                                   ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  05  ANALYSIS                                                ║
║      Apply frame analysis, geolocation, and timeline         ║
║      techniques to extract key indicators                    ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  06  FINDINGS                                                ║
║      Establish authenticity, location, and timeline with     ║
║      supporting evidence                                     ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  07  INTELLIGENCE ASSESSMENT                                 ║
║      Assess confidence level and alternative explanations    ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  08  REPORT                                                  ║
║      Document conclusions in a reproducible format           ║
║                                                              ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

Every conclusion is evidence-backed. Every step is reproducible.
